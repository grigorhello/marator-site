Frequently asked questions

Quick answers. No fluff.

Do I need marathon experience?
No. Marator is designed to start from your current level and build gradually.
What if I miss a session?
The plan recalculates so you can continue safely without restarting.
Can I train around other sports?
Yes. You can plan around activities like soccer, gym, or rest days.
